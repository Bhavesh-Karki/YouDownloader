How to Use YT Downloader


Step 1: Copy Video Link


Go to YouTube and find the video you want to download. Copy the URL from your browser's address bar or by clicking the "Share" button.


Step 2: Paste the Link


Return to YT Downloader and paste the copied link into the search box on the main page. Click the "Download" button.


Step 3: Choose Format


Our system will process the video and provide you with multiple download options. Select your desired video quality (e.g., 1080p, 720p) or choose the Audio Only (MP3) option.


Step 4: Save & Enjoy


Your download will start automatically. And You can find your downloaded file in your system's downloads folder!
